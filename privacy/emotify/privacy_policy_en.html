Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2, 2026

1. Information Collection

Emotify ("the App") respects and protects your personal privacy. The App itself does not require you to create an account and does not proactively upload the photos, videos, GIFs, or Live Photos you select to our servers.

During use, the App may request access to the following device features:

  • Photo Library: To select images or videos as sticker materials
  • Camera: To capture photos or videos (if applicable)
  • Local Storage: To save generated GIF files and history records

In addition, to support ad delivery, consent management, and service reliability, third-party services integrated into the App may process certain device- or advertising-related data, such as device identifiers, IP address, diagnostic information, performance data, and ad interaction data. Such processing is governed by the policies of those third-party service providers.

2. Use of Information

All media files accessed by the App are processed entirely on your local device and are never uploaded to any server. Generated GIFs and history records are stored locally on your device only.

Data processed by third-party advertising and privacy-compliance services is primarily used for ad delivery, ad frequency control, consent management, troubleshooting, and improving service reliability.

3. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device, including:

  • Generated GIF and image sticker files
  • History records
  • User preferences (such as language settings)

For data involved in advertising and consent-compliance flows, storage and processing may be handled by the relevant third-party providers in accordance with their own privacy policies.

4. Information Sharing and Third-Party Processing

The App does not sell the media content you select and does not upload that content to our servers. When you use the "Share" feature, content is sent through the system share sheet, and the App does not perform additional tracking of that sharing action.

When providing ad delivery and consent management, Google AdMob and Google's User Messaging Platform (UMP) may process necessary data in accordance with their own product and privacy policies. In-app purchases are provided and processed by Apple's official payment system.

5. Third-Party Services

The App integrates Google AdMob to display app open ads and uses Google's User Messaging Platform (UMP) to manage advertising consent in supported regions. Ads are shown only to users who have not purchased Pro, and Pro removes ads.

In-app purchases are handled by Apple's official payment system. The App does not upload the photos, videos, GIFs, or Live Photos you select to our servers.

For more information about how these third-party services handle data, you may refer to their official privacy materials:

  • Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Google Ads Technologies: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads
  • Apple Privacy Policy: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

6. Your Rights

You can manage your data at any time through the following means:

  • Delete generated GIFs from the "History" section
  • Revoke photo library or camera access in your device's Settings
  • Manage advertising privacy options from the in-app Privacy page, where applicable and when required for your region
  • Uninstalling the App will remove all local data
